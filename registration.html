The Saints - na Naomh

New Players

Cleveland Gaelic Athletic Association (GAA) is comprised of four teams: Men's Football, Ladies' Football, Hurling, and Camogie. Competing within the USGAA's Midwest Division, Cleveland GAA faces opponents in Buffalo, Cinncinnatti, Detroit, and Pittsburgh. The club's home pitch, Pairc na Naomh, is located at the West Side Irish American Club in Olmsted Township.
